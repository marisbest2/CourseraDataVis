The top chart shows Wikipedia connections between presidents of the United States

Mousing over a president will show connections with sources in green and targets in red

The bottom chart shows connections between sporting events in a different format

There are some cool connections, and constrasts. For example, compare the two Bush presidents and see that W. is usually a source while Bush Sr. is usually a target. Also notice that international sporting events tend to be more highly connected than USA events like the Super Bowl
