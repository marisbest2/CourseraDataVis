The charts below show the tempratures in each specified region and the number of years that region was the hotest/coldest

Toggle the buttons to show/hide that series data. The bar charts have mouseover information

Overall, the charts show that contrary to what we would have thought, any region can be the hottest/coldest.

Additionally, we see clear evidence of the warming of the earth over time.
